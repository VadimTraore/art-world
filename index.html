What for ?

Art-world is a website designed to bring the world of art closer to everyone. Through an easy-to-navigate platform, users can explore various artistic movements, learn about their unique characteristics, and gain a deeper appreciation for the diversity of art history. Art-world is here to make art accessible to all, encouraging curiosity and discovery in a creative and educational environment.

By who ?
